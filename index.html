Lavender Care Guide

Soil for Lavender

Well-Draining, Sandy Soil

Use well-draining, slightly alkaline soil like sandy or loamy soil.

Watering

Infrequent Watering

Water sparingly, allowing the soil to dry out between waterings.

Pot Size

Medium-Sized Pot

Choose a pot that offers ample space for root growth without excess.

Colors

Lavender flowers come in various hues like purple, yellow, and white.

Benefits

Aromatic Properties

Lavender emits a delightful fragrance used in aromatherapy and relaxation.

Medicinal Uses

It's known for its calming and antiseptic properties, used in various remedies.

Non-Benefits

Overwatering can lead to root rot and damage for Lavender plants.
